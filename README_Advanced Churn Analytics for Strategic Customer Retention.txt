# Advanced Churn Analytics for Strategic Customer Retention

## 📊 Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides an in-depth analysis of customer churn to support strategic retention initiatives. By visualizing key metrics such as churn rate, payment preferences, contract types, and customer exit patterns, the dashboard helps stakeholders identify high-risk segments and make data-driven decisions.

## 🔍 Key Insights

- **Churn Rate**: 26.5% churn observed from a total of 7,043 customers.
- **Gender Distribution**: Nearly equal representation of male and female customers.
- **Contract Type Impact**: Month-to-month contracts are significantly more prone to churn.
- **Payment Method Influence**: Customers using electronic checks have the highest churn rate.
- **Tenure & Charges**: Customers with shorter tenure and lower monthly charges are more likely to churn.
